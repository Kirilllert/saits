Small strawberry and
pistachio pavlova

An argument dating back generations
between Australia and New Zealand over
which of them invented the pavlova appears
to have finally been settled – by the Oxford
English Dictionary.

  The first recorded pavlova recipe appeared
in New Zealand in 1927 in "Davis Dainty
Dishes"  Both countries regard the sweet
meringue-based, cream and fruit topped
dish as their "national" dessert, but the
identity of its creator is a source of intense dispute.
